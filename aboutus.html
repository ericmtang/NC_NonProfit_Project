The Mission of Leeward Youth Voyages

As a nonprofit aimed at expanding environmental education, our goal is to provide students from all socioeconomic backgrounds with an all-expenses paid educational experience abroad. We fascilitate and foster interest in marine conservation and environmental justice in our students, knowing that they are the future caretakers of our oceans.
